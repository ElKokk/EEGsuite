# F2HEAL: Advancing Parkinson’s Research through Innovation

Worldwide, approximately **10 million people** suffer from Parkinson’s disease, including an estimated **30,000 cases in Flanders**. Despite ongoing research, Western medicine has yet to find a cure or determine the exact cause of the disease.

## Our Visionaries
**Pieter Godefroy**, a lifelong dreamer, visionary, and inventor, was diagnosed with Parkinson’s himself. Along with his brother **Wouter** — both engineers and entrepreneurs — they are using their expertise to improve the quality of life for those affected by the condition.

## The Mission
In their spare time, they research and develop innovative ways to alleviate symptoms through the application of **vibrations on the body**. Their initiative, **F2HEAL**, serves as a research club dedicated to exploring and advancing Parkinson’s research.

### Commitment to Open Collaboration
F2HEAL transforms scientific insights into accessible knowledge and technology, striving to enhance global health outcomes for individuals with Parkinson’s. Acting as both an **accelerator** and a **catalyst**, the group aims to drive meaningful progress in the field.

*   They freely share their knowledge and experience on a **non-profit basis** with anyone interested or in need.
*   Currently, they are collaborating on a research project with **Neurotech Leuven**.
